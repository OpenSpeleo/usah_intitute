Our Mission

We are dedicated to exploring, documenting, and protecting underwater caves and archaeological sites in Mexico, Florida, and beyond. Our work preserves humanity's earliest traces and educates communities on the cultural and environmental significance of these natural wonders.

Our founders are divers, scientists, and conservationists who believe that ancient history—hidden in the depths—deserves to be seen, studied, and safeguarded.

What We Do

Exploration

We dive into uncharted underwater cave systems to uncover and map lost heritage.

Conservation

We advocate for protection of critical sites through data, policy, and partnerships.
